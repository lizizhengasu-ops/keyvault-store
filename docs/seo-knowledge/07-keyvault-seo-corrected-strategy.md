# KeyVault SEO Full Cycle Strategy

> Sources: 大罗SEO Lessons 1/3/4/5/6 + Ahrefs 7 Tips + Backlinko Cases

## Core Principle

80% of SEO results come from the first 20% of work done in the early stage. The middle and late stages are maintenance only. This is validated by:
- 大罗SEO Lesson 1,3,4,5,6 - all core SEO work is front-loaded
- Ahrefs 7 Tips for Small Business - 5 out of 7 are one-time early tasks
- Backlinko case study - success came from early SEO focus

| Phase | Time | Effort | Nature |
|-------|------|--------|--------|
| Early | Week 1-8 | 80% | One-time build (tech/keywords/content/B2B/links) |
| Mid | Week 8-16 | 15% | Maintenance (refresh/follow-up/reports/tweaks) |
| Late | Week 16+ | 5% | Strategy adjustment (GEO/new directions/cleanup) |

---

## Phase 1: Early (Week 1-8, 80% effort)

Source: 大罗SEO Lesson 3 (keywords are the foundation), Lesson 4 (On-Page during content),
Lesson 5 (start content early), Lesson 6 (technical SEO is one-time)
Ahrefs 7 Tips: 5 out of 7 are one-time early tasks

### Week 1: Technical (one-time)
Ref: 大罗SEO Lesson 6 - crawl + index
- Domain + HTTPS online
- Start SEO Kit (server.js + HTTP service)
- Google Search Console + Bing Webmaster
- robots.txt + sitemap.xml
- 9 product pages + 4 category pages returning 200

### Week 2: On-Page (one-time)
Ref: 大罗SEO Lesson 4 + Ahrefs Title templates
- Every product page: Title 30-65 chars, Desc 50-165 chars
- Product descriptions: 1 sentence -> 200-500 words
- JSON-LD Product Schema (SEO Kit auto-generates)
- Internal linking system

### Week 3: Keywords + Directories (one-time)
Ref: 大罗SEO Lesson 3 + Ahrefs Tips 2+6
- B2B keywords: 10-15 (bulk/wholesale/volume/enterprise)
- B2C keywords: 15-20 (buy/cheap/activate/license)
- Guide/compare keywords: 10-15 (how to/vs/best)
- Submit: Trustpilot + Sitejabber + GBP
- Start product feedback + where to buy outreach

### Week 4-8: Content batch build (10-15 articles)
Ref: 大罗SEO Lesson 5 + Backlinko case (0->180k/mo via early SEO)

Not 1 article per week. Batch 10-15 articles in 4 weeks. Content must exist to rank.

| Batch | Week | Content | Type |
|-------|------|---------|------|
| 1 | 4 | Windows 11 Pro vs Home comparison | Compare |
| 1 | 4 | OEM vs Retail keys explained | Educational |
| 1 | 4 | How to activate Windows 11 | Tutorial |
| 2 | 5 | Win10 vs Win11 which is right | Compare |
| 2 | 5 | Best Windows key buying guide 2026 | List |
| 2 | 5 | Bulk Windows purchasing for business | B2B |
| 3 | 6 | Install and activate Office 2019 | Tutorial |
| 3 | 6 | Office 2019 vs M365 comparison | Compare |
| 4 | 7-8 | FAQ/case studies/trends (5-7 more) | Mixed |

### B2B Conversion Pages (parallel, one-time)
- /b2b: cases + testimonials + tiered pricing + bulk inquiry
- Contact form: name/company/WeChat/requirements
- Buying guide PDF download (requires WeChat/email)
- FAQ 10-15 questions + FAQPage Schema
- B2B CTA at bottom of every article

### Early Stage Completion Checklist
- Search Console: > 0 indexed
- seoKit.report(): avgScore >= 80
- seoKit.validate(): all passing
- 10-15 articles published
- B2B conversion funnel working

## Phase 2: Mid (Week 8-16, 15% effort)

Core: NOT building new things. Maintaining what was built in early stage.

| Task | Frequency | What to do |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Content refresh | Every 2 weeks | Update existing articles (extend + optimize), NOT write new |
| Link follow-up | As needed | Reply to product feedback emails + monitor unlinked mentions |
| Review management | Auto + manual | Post-purchase review invites + respond (E-E-A-T signal) |
| Data analysis | Every 2 weeks | seoKit.report() + Search Console check |
| Small tweaks | As needed | Optimize Title wording / update keywords.json |

---

## Phase 3: Late (Week 16+, 5% effort)

Core: Only enter this phase after early+mid are stable. 1-2 hours per week max.

| Task | Frequency | What to do |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Strategy tweak | Monthly | Has impressions no clicks -> optimize Title / 6mo no traffic -> 301 |
| AI Search (GEO) | Explore | Content structure / LLMs.txt / Entity SEO |
| New directions | On demand | Multi-language? Video? Based on data |


## Effort Comparison

| Phase | Hours/week | % of total |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Early (Week 1-8) | 10-15 hours | 80% |
| Mid (Week 8-16) | 2-3 hours | 15% |
| Late (Week 16+) | 1-2 hours | 5% |
